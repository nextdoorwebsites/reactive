Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our treatments.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your specific condition, its severity, and how your body responds to treatment. After your initial assessment, your physiotherapist will provide a personalized timeline.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide variety of conditions including sports injuries, back and neck pain, joint arthritis, post-surgical recovery, posture-related aches, and more.

Do you offer laser therapy?

Yes, we are equipped with advanced laser therapy devices. Laser therapy is excellent for reducing inflammation, promoting tissue repair, and providing rapid pain relief without medication.

Can physiotherapy help chronic pain?

Absolutely. Physiotherapy is one of the most effective ways to manage and often resolve chronic pain through targeted strengthening, mobility exercises, and advanced modalities.
